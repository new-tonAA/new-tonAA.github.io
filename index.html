About the Conference

ACM MM 2026 is a prestigious international conference that brings together experts and researchers in multimedia technologies. The conference will feature state-of-the-art research on topics including machine learning, computer vision, and data analysis.

Important Dates

  • Submission Deadline: July 1, 2026
  • Notification of Acceptance: August 1, 2026
  • Conference Dates: October 10-12, 2026

Call for Papers

We invite researchers and professionals to submit their papers for the ACM MM 2026 conference. Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Audio and Speech Processing

  • AI in Multimedia
